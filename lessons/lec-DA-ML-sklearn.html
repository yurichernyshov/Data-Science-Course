"", true positive (TP), false positive (FP)
"", false negative (FN), true negative (TN)

Accuracy

$$ Accuracy = \frac{tp+tn}{tp+fp+fn+tn} $$

Precision

$$ Precision = \frac{tp}{tp+fp} $$

Recall

$$ Recall = \frac{tp}{tp+fn} $$$$ F_\beta = (1+\beta^2) \frac{p r}{\beta ^2 p + r},~F_1 = \frac{2pr}{p+r} $$

Метрики для задачи регрессии ¶

Mean Square Error (MSE)¶

$l$ - , $y_i$ - , $\hat{y_i}$ - .

.

$$ L = \frac{1}{2}\sum_{i=1}^{n}{\left(y_i - \hat{y_i}\right)^2} $$

logloss¶

$l$ - , $y_i$ - , $\hat{y_i}$ - .

$$ L = \frac{1}{l}\sum_{i=1}^{l}{\left( y_i \log{\hat{y_i}} - (1-y_i)\log{(1-\hat{y_i})} \right)} $$
